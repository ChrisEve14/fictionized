We are a community of readers and writers who bring characters and stories to life as we create our own roleplaying world together.

All characters, plotlines, and settings are original and belong to the writers. While the stories might take inspiration from real-life scenarios or fictional ones portrayed in movies or shows, we do not have any affiliations with actors/actresses used in role-playing or have any rights to actual locations or media that is posted on the platforms unless it is stated by the owner (manipulations, edits, etc.)

Fictionized encourages readers to become writers and take it a step further as they become their own characters and interact with others. Readers may also remain in such a role and engage in conversations with other readers and characters to feel like part of the story as never before.

We expect every member to conduct themselves with kindness, acts of bullying or trolling will be not tolerated.
